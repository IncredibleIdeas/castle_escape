### Game Locations

The castle features 10 interconnected rooms:

1. **Entrance Hall** - Starting point with a Torch
2. **Courtyard** - Central area with multiple exits
3. **Guard Tower** - High vantage point with a Crowbar
4. **Library** - Contains books and a Rusty Key
5. **Kitchen** - Find Healing Herb here
6. **Armory** - Locked room containing a Sword
7. **Study** - Contains a mysterious Note
8. **Dungeon** - Dark area requiring light
9. **Secret Passage** - Hidden route to throne room
10. **Throne Room** - Final destination with the Golden Crown

### Items to Collect

1. **Torch** - Essential for navigating dark areas
2. **Rusty Key** - Unlocks the Armory
3. **Crowbar** - Can open hidden passages
4. **Healing Herb** - Restores health and helps NPCs
5. **Golden Crown** - Victory item (win condition)
6. **Sword** - Found in the Armory
7. **Note** - Provides clues about secrets

### Strategic Tips

- **Inventory Management:** You can only carry 4 items at once. Choose wisely!
- **Dark Areas:** The Dungeon requires a Torch to explore
- **Locked Doors:** The Armory needs the Rusty Key from the Library
- **NPC Interaction:** Talking to characters can provide valuable clues
- **Item Usage:** Some items have specific uses in particular locations
